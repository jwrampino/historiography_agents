For Y'ur Height Only (For Your Height Only) is a 1981 Phillipine movie starring 2' 9" tall Weng Weng as Agent 00. Agent 00 must save the world from various nefarious baddies. He beats them up (using his signature punch to the balls), and lovely normal-sized Fillipinas swoon. Many consider this to be one of the classic cult movies. If you only watch one Phillipine midget spy movie this year, make it this one. It's in color and in English.
