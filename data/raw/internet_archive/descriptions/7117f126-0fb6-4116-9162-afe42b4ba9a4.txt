Korean Central Television &nbsp;( KCTV ;&nbsp; Korean :&nbsp; 조선중앙텔레비죤 ;&nbsp; MR :&nbsp; Chosŏn Chungang T'ellebijyon ) is a North Korean television service operated by the&nbsp; Korean Central Broadcasting Committee , a&nbsp; state-owned broadcaster &nbsp;in&nbsp; North Korea . It is broadcast terrestrially via the&nbsp; Pyongyang TV Tower &nbsp;in&nbsp; Moranbong-guyok ,&nbsp; Pyongyang , streamed via the government-run&nbsp; internet television &nbsp;service&nbsp; Manbang , and also uplinked via satellite.
