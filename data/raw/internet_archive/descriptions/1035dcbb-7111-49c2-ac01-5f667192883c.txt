Wellcome Collection holds one of the greatest specialist collections on the history of medicine and the medical humanities. It houses over 1.1 million items of extraordinary range and diversity, including books, films, archives, manuscripts and artworks from around the world. Wellcome Collection is making as many of its collections as possible freely available online. In addition to this selection of historical books and journals, increasing amounts of digitised material, including manuscripts and archive collections can be found on the Wellcome Collection website.&nbsp; Wellcome Collection is part of the Wellcome Trust , a global charitable foundation dedicated to achieving extraordinary improvements in human and animal health.
