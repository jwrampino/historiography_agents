These silent Super 8 videos are from 1968 to 1970. They are of a safari in Kenya and tours of England, Germany, Greece and Cambodia and the surrounding areas around 1969. My mother was a teacher for the DOD at the Air Force Base in Okinawa and at the Air Force Base in Schweinfurt Germany.  Many of these landmarks were damaged during warring as well. Angkor Wat Temples still remain and tell of the life of the great kings of the 12th and 13th century. She told me and my sister many stories of her life teaching overseas before she was married. They were full of adventure and excitement. I cannot remember most of these places names and she passed away 4 years ago. If you know of these places or recognize them feel free to add your own narration.
