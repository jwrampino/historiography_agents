Channel Description: Channel 10 was a discontinued broadcast network based in Manila, Philippines. Planned to launch initially in 1983, the opening was moved to September 1988. After just 2 months, the network was shut down for undisclosed reasons without any announcements. However, employees who asked not to be named shared that the Channel 10 frequency was cursed.  Source: Wikiphedia The channel can be found at https://youtube.com/@Channel10Manila?si=kiXkGR_z7jZKoKFA
