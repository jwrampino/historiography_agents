The 1987 published book, 'The Murder of Little Mary Phagan' written by the Grand-Niece and namesake of the victim, Mary Phagan-Kean (Born June 5th, 1954). This book was transformed into an audiobook in 2015 with permission, for the centennial of Leo Frank's lynching (1915-2015) and distributed by The American Mercury. The American Mercury has made the audiobook public domain. Permission is granted for it to be downloaded and distributed.  This audiobook is under threat of censorship, please download each part and make it available on other media websites, so that others might learn the inside story of the Leo Frank case that has been suppressed since 1913.
