The Anna Centenary Library (ACL), was established by the Government of Tamil Nadu on September 15, 2010 on the occasion of the 102nd birth anniversary of the former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Dr. C.N. Annadurai. At present, ACL hosts five lakh books covering a wide range of subjects to serve the information needs of the public, academic and corporate community. ACL has a Braille Section, Own Books Reading Section, Children Section, Periodicals and Newspapers Section, Tamil books Section, English Books Section, etc.&nbsp; On behalf of the Directorate of Public Libraries, Anna Centenary Library has created a digital library repository consisting of rare books, periodicals and palm-leaf manuscripts. Anna Centenary Library digitized several rare materials from the Connemara Public Library, Tamil Nadu Archive Library, Department of Archaeology, Department of Information & Public Relations, Directorate of Indian Medicine & Homeopathy, Madurai Kamaraj University, Annamalai University, Presidency College, Tamil Music College, Madurai Tamil Sangam, etc. The library is being enriched continuously by uploading more rare content.
