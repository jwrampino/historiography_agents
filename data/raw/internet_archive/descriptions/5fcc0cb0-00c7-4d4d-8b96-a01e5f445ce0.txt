Veterinary Record (currently branded as Vet Record ) is an active peer-reviewed scholarly journal currently published semi-monthly by Wiley. Founded in 1888 by British veterinary surgeon William Hunting (1846–1913), it is the official journal of&nbsp; the British Veterinary Association (BVA) and provides coverage of current research and developments in the field of veterinary medicine. Each issue features original research papers and communications covering a wide range of veterinary topics, including veterinary practice, animal welfare, veterinary education, ethics, professional issues, One Health, and Public health, among others. It also publishes the latest industry news and opinion pieces, together with disease surveillance reports, letters and notices, obituaries, and careers information, as well as reviews of books, diagnostic devices and any product that assists vet professionals in their working lives.
