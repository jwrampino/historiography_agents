The document "2025 Mandate For Leadership" outlines a conservative vision for governance, focusing on policy recommendations across various sectors such as defense, homeland security, education, energy, health and human services, housing, and labor, among others. It suggests reforms aimed at reducing federal government size and influence, enhancing national security, promoting free-market principles, and safeguarding individual liberties. The text includes contributions from various authors aligned with conservative organizations, offering a comprehensive roadmap for a conservative administration to implement starting January 2025.
