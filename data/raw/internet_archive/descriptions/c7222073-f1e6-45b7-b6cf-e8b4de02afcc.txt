"This illustrated gazetteer, running into fourteen volumes, presents a detailed description of the peoples of all nations in their habits as they live. Tracing their racial origins it discusses at length their history, their manners and customs. Enriched with 111 pages of coloured photographs and 12 pages of coloured maps, it arranges the nations of world in alphabetical order." (jacket) Volume Contents: Vol. I.
