LibriVox volunteers bring you 7 recordings of Epilogue by Bliss Carman. This was the Fortnightly Poetry project for February 21, 2016. Read in English by Brian Darby; David Lawrence; Newgatenovelist; Greg Giordano; Leonard Wilson; Maria Kasper and Peter Yearsley. In 1904, Canadian poet Bliss Carman published Sappho: One Hundred Lyrics, which was not just a translation of the fragments but an imaginative reconstruction of the lost poems. While of little to no scholarly value, Carman's translations brought Sappho's work to the attention of a wide readership. Our Fortnightly Poem is the Epilogue to this collection by Bliss Carman.
