I had discovered this neat little movie a few years ago on the Archive, but the visual quality wasn't great, and while the audio was in English, it had Chinese subtitles. Despite this being the only version I could find, I was always on the lookout for better versions. Then a few months ago, I discovered a VHS copy of this movie that had very good visual quality, but had French audio. So I took it upon myself to sort of combine the two sources to make this. It required a bit of editing, and playing with framerates, but I'd say it worked pretty well. Until whoever owns the Filmation archives decides to release a remastered version of this movie, I guess this is about as good as it's going to get. Hope you enjoy it!
