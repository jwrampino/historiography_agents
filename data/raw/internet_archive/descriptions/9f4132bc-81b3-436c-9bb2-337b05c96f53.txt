My HD reconstruction of "The Return of Godzilla" in its English international export version, what I call the EOST (English on Screen Text) version. Utilizing many sources, such as the Kraken Blu-ray of the Japanese cut, a TV Broadcast of the American version, a 1998 PAL VHS, a German LaserDisc and some custom mattes, I present, for the first time in high definition, the EOST version of Toho's 1984 reboot to the Godzilla franchise, The Return of Godzilla.
