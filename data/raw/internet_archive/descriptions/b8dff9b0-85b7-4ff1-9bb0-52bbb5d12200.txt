The following collection is pretty much a near complete filmography of Walt Disney animation studio cartoons from 1928 to 1969. But not just that there are a few extra shorts taken from different sources Post 1969 like Tim Burton Vincent, and once upon a studio to name a few. All the cartoons were collected from different Internet archive sources, YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion and cartoon sites since this collection is mostly a personal set, there are a few odds and ends that were thrown in like parody films like suicide mouse and Mickey goes to Vietnam to be a completist lol There are other stuff like both goofy movies, Walt Disney Christmas shows from 1950 and 1951, Ward Kimball man in space trilogy, and the 1943 film "victory through air power" I hope you enjoy this personal collection of a near complete filmography of Walt Disney animation Studios.
