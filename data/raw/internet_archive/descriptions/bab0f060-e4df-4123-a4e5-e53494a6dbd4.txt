Music of the Moken People An Austronesian ethnic group with about 2,500 members who maintain a nomadic, sea-based culture, living on sea, shores, and islands of southeast Asia; from the Mergui archipelago in Burma, to the Sula Sea in the Phillipines, as well as the coasts of Malaysia and Indonesia; with the majority living around southern Burma and southern Thailand. This song is a lament for the devastation caused by the tsunami of 2004. recorded in Bangkok, Thailand at the Thailand Cultural Center 07/05/2010
