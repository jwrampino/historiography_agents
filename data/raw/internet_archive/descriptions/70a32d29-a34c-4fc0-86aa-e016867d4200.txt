After World War II stories emerged of vast amounts of gold bullion looted by the Japanese, and buried somewhere beneath the Philippine hills. This three part documentary tells the true story of what happened when US intelligence recovered the treasure, and why mentioning the words 'Golden Lily' can be a dangerous business, even today. Includes an interview with Peggy Seagrave, co-author of 'Gold Warriors: America's Secret Recovery of Yamashita's Gold'.
