2010 U.S. Open champion Graeme McDowell joined me in South Korea for a lengthy chat about his career, the status of his game, trying to keep up with the long hitters, and his future in golf. Graeme reflected on his recent struggles, the mental battles he's been facing, and how family life has changed where golf ranks on his priority list. Graeme is a pretty fascinating guy, and we could not be more appreciative of the time.
