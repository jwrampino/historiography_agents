ICIS Chemical Business is currently an online magazine focusing on the news and analysis of the global chemical business market. Until 2006 ICIS Chemical Business was known and printed as the Chemical Market Reporter, a monthly magazine. The Chemical Market Reporter’s focus was in providing an in-depth insight into chemical price changes and industry trends, aiming to enable its readers to make strategic decisions, mitigate risk, improve productivity and capitalise on new opportunities.
