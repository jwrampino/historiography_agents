The National Library of Cuba José Martí (Spanish: Biblioteca Nacional de Cuba José Martí) is the national library in Cuba. It is located in La Habana and named after the national hero José Martí. This library was established in October 18, 1901. It coordinates the network of Cuban public libraries and is responsible for the preservation of the national document heritage of the country.
