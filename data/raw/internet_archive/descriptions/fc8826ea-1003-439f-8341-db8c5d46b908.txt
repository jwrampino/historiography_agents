James N. Mason (former neo-nazi, and fan of Charles Manson), published a newsletter titled SIEGE in the early 1980s. In 1992, portions of that newsletter were used in the book 'Siege: The Collected Writings of James Mason.' In it, Mason advocated leaderless resistance, calling for autonomous action by individuals rather than an authoritarian hierarchical organization.
