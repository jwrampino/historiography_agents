In a country populated by immigrants, why are Americans so wary of newcomers? What's the difference between the nativism of the early Republic and and the anti-immigrant sentiment on talk radio today? How do we draw the line between "us" and "them?" Frank Morris of the Center for Immigration Studies discusses historic tensions between immigrant laborers and African-Americans.
