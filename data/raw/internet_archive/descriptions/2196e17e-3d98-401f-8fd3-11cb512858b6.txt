Here are all the known episodes that exist of M&M Communications' short lived dub of the classic Mazinger Z anime. This dub (alongside a dub from the Phillipines that continued off of the M&M dub) was commissioned directly from Toei around 1977-1978 for an attempt to take a crack at the English speaking market. Thanks to Nanto of the Skaro Hunting Society, Beta8 and Chili for remastering the first 2 episodes with DVD quality footage.  Known voice cast: Koji Kabuto: Dando Kluever Shiro Kabuto: Dana Ikeda Sayaka Yumi: Priscilla Piano Professor Yumi: Dale Hemmond Baron Ashler (Male): William Saylor Baron Ashler (Female): Elisabeth Wichman Iron Mask: Robin Gould Doctor Hell: Dale Hemmond Woodstick: Frank Cattelano
