In response to MrColeslaw of OriginalTrilogy.com working on a reconstruction for the show, I should help out as well. These not only come from TVRips, but also VHS tapes released in the US, UK and AU, as well as DVDs by Toy Play. Some episodes may be low in quality, (audio at the most), but they all come with their original "Go Bye-Bye" blackouts! The UK releases are from Children's Video Library and Columbia Tristar, and the AU releases are CEL. Plus, a few of my encodes are from the VHS releases and LaserDisc, too. Credit goes to the guys who encoded the shows. Also, due to the sluggish upload speed on this site and occasional network problems I get, I will upload one season at a time.
