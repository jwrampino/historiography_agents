This is an educational game which uses time travel and memory drills to teach history. The program is designed so that you can alter the questions to teach or memorize any history period or subject. Upon selecting a time period you will travel back in time. Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to find the Crystal Key of Time. It is imperative that the key be found before history is changed forever. If the key gets into the wrong hands it will change the balance of power, giving the key holder the power to control the world, thus becoming the first world dictator. You will be given 1000 pieces of gold, guard it well. There are many pitfalls and unfriendly travelers that will steal your gold. There are also friendly travelers who may give you gold. You do not know what any other portal holds. It could be goodies or trouble.
