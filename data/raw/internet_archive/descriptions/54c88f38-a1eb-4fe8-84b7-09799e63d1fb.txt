Foreword going on living: Lore Segal -- Vienna -- Camps: Ghetto -- Death camp -- Forced labor camp -- Three Germany: Escape -- Defeated country -- New York -- Epilogue Swept up as a child in the events of Nazi-era Europe, Kluger saw her family's comfortable Vienna existence destroyed. Despite her shattered childhood, Kluger eventually reclaimed her life. A coming-of-age story that delves into the unsentimental observations of childhood, "Still Alive" rejects easy assumptions about history as Kluger relates how she and her family survived the Holocaust
