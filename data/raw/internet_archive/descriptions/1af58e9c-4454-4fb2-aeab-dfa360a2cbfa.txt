This episode of KOOP Radio's  People United  features historian Madeline Y. Hsu on her book  The Good Immigrants: How the Yellow Peril Became the Model Minority , an examination of the historical relationship between changes in US policies and perceptions of Chinese immigrants before the COVID-19 pandemic.  She spoke at the Asian American Resource Center in Austin, Texas, on September 12, 2015.  This program's coverage was originally broadcast on October 23, 2015.
