A woman's lust for money leads her dentist husband to commit murder. Directed by Oscar-nominee actor/director Erich von Stroheim ("Sunset Boulevard"). Despite being cut from its original eight hour length, it remains one of the greatest silent films ever produced. This is a  fanmade  reconstruction of the original release version that audiences saw back in 1924;  It uses high quality footage from  Rick Schmidlin's 4-hour reconstruction of the film  available on iTunes   and it's complemented with intertitles and bits from   a VHS print released by MGM in 1989. Carl Davis' score was removed from this online upload to avoid copyright strikes, although it may be useful as means to claim a decent home-video release of the film ( now that it is in the Public Domain ) .
