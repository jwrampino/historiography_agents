LibriVox  volunteers bring you 11 recordings of A Mystery by John Greenleaf Whittier.  This was the Fortnightly Poetry project for March 12, 2017. Read in English by Algy Pug; Bruce Kachuk; Newgatenovelist; Garth Burton; Gloria Begemann; Greg Giordano; KyoBlanchett; Michael Fassio; SaraHale; Tomas Peter and tovarisch. John Greenleaf Whittier was an American Quaker poet and advocate of the abolition of slavery in the United States. Frequently listed as one of the Fireside Poets, he was influenced by the Scottish poet Robert Burns. Whittier is remembered particularly for his anti-slavery writings as well as his book Snow-Bound.
