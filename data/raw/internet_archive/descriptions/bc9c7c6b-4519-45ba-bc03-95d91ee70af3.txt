This page contains bibliographies and other resources for Terrorism Research on a range of subjects compiled by Judith Tinnes for the scholarly journal Perspectives on Terrorism (PT) – between 2013 and 2023. The page is intended to provide users with a single entry point to download the bibliographies. Users can either download particular bibliographies in PDF or other formats or download the full set in a single ZIP archive (see the file inventory on the right). For bibliographies published since 2024 visit: https://archive.org/details/terrorism-research-bibliographies-2
