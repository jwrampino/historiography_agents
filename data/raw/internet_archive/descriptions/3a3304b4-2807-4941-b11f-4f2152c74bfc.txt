LibriVox recording of Epochs of American History: Division and Reunion 1829-1889 by Thomas Woodrow Wilson. Read in English by Jim Locke. This third volume of Epochs of American History covers a tumultuous sixty years between the presidencies of Andrew Jackson and Grover Cleveland. It was a time of evolution through the conflict of political and racial ideologies and resolutions best symbolized by the Civil War and Reconstruction.
