This playlist contains videos with various topics by Thulean Perspective (Varg Vikernes, known from the band Burzum) from 2016. Music clips, the series on paganism and videos about his role playing game Myfarog is not included here. This is Varg's takes on paganism, philosophy, democracy and modern society.
