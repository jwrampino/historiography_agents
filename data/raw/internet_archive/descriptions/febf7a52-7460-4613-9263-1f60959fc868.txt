This special panel discussion on the Chinese Exclusion Act was presented December 11, 2021 at the Cupertino Community Hall. Featured speakers include Professor Gordon Chang of Stanford University, Executive Director of the Angel Island Immigration Station Foundation Edward Tepporn, and Author Connie Young Yu. Vice Chair of the Cupertino Parks & Recreation Commission Minna Xu moderates the event with introductory and concluding remarks by Cupertino Mayor Darcy Paul.
