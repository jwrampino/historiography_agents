This is an archive of all the first 6 seasons in the highest quality I could find, compiled from various different sources. These serials have been fan-edited with my own English subtitles for those who are hearing impaired.  This collection is still ongoing and most 60's serials have been subtitled so far.  Due to the scale of the project, this is a huge task only being undertaken by myself (and I'm only human!) so I'm grateful for all of your patience.  I will update this archive respectively as I complete each serial.   In recognition of copyright, I do not own, nor have I created anything depicted in each episode (apart from my subtitles).
