LibriVox recording of The Long Road of Woman's Memory by Jane Addams. Read in English by Ciufi Galeazzi In this book Jane Addams tells of a strange rumor involving Hull House, the famed settlement house founded by her in Chicago in 1889. The rumor had arisen that a "Devil Baby" was residing there, and for a brief period of time many women came to Hull House hoping to see this child of legend. The women shared stories with Addams about their own often tragic lives, and prompted her to write this book delving into the role of myth and painful memory in the lives of women. Addams is revered for her work at Hull house, and was a prominent sociologist, activist, and author. She was a recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize and co-founder of the American Civil Liberties Union. - Summary by Ciufi Galeazzi For further information, including links to online text, reader information, RSS feeds, CD cover or other formats (if available), please go to the LibriVox catalog page for this recording.
