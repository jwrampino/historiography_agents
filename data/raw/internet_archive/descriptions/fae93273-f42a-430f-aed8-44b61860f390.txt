LibriVox recording of El Dorado, by Baroness Emmuska Orczy. Read by Karen Savage. El Dorado, by Baroness Orczy is a sequel book to the classic adventure tale, The Scarlet Pimpernel. It was first published in 1913. The novel is notable in that it is the partial basis for most of the film treatments of the original book.
