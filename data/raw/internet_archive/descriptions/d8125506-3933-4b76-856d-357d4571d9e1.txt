Everton's Genealogical Helper was a publication covering the field of genealogy. It was established in 1947 and was published bimonthly by Everton Publishers until 2009. The publication aimed to provide genealogical enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals with valuable insights, resources, and information to assist them in their pursuit of tracing family histories and understanding ancestral connections. It features research papers, case studies, methodological approaches, and comparative analyses, offering practical guidance on utilizing various genealogical resources such as census records, vital records, immigration records, military records, and more.
