This collection consists of more than 3,000 audio cassette tapes and 5,000+ CDRs and CDs of 20th c. Contemporary Classic music by Latin American and Hispanic composers and performers. Most of these composers are working in established traditions and represent modern, but not avant-garde works. &nbsp;Many of these recordings were taped off the air and represent rare premieres by East Coast orchestras and ensembles that are otherwise unavailable. Most were obtained through direct contact with the composers and performers. All materials are catalogued electronically and given an accession number by the collector. Mr. Pogonyi (1946 - 2017) resided in Philadelphia, PA, and devoted much of his live to the study and preservation of Classical music. He came to America in 1956, a refugee from the Hungarian Revolution, and earned a PhD from U. of New Mexico in Latin American Economic History. &nbsp;He was the author of The Search for Trade and Profits in Bourbon Colombia, 1765-1777, and an active contributor to publications researching Classical material, including Discography Music for Piano and Orchestra. by Dr. Allan B. Ho and Italian, Portuguese, Spanish And Latin American Symphonies by Michael Herman. &nbsp;Mr. Herman has said, “Miklos Pogonyi … whose broad knowledge of recordings was totally indispensable for this project to reach its completion.” Link to donation: https://archive.org/details/miklospogonyi-20170412 Photo from https://www.legacy.com/obituaries/name/miklos-pogonyi-obituary?pid=185209143
