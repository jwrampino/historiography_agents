Volume 77 no. 1 includes "Standing Tall," article about opening of a limb reconstruction clinic founded by University of Maryland professors. Volume 77 no. 2 includes announcement of Charles E. Silberstein and Sheldon Goldgeier as team physicians for the Orioles, and editorial with photographs of the World War II medical units reunion hosted by George Yeager '29. Volume 77 no. 3 includes editorial on Dr. Andrew Rudo and Dr. Jon Book (class of '75) that started a rock band "Shrink the Deficit." Volume 78 no. 1 includes announcement and sketch of the future Health Sciences Facility. Volume 78 no. 2 includes article on Dr. Ellen Silbergeld receiving the MacArthur award. Volume 79 no. 2 includes article about Robert Berkow (class of '53) celebrating 20 years as editor of the Merck Manual. Volume 79 no. 3 includes announcement and photographs of the new Homer Gudelsky Building. (v. 77, no. 1 (Summer 1992)- v. 79, no. 4 (Spring 1995)).
