A collection of every english-dubbed episode of Toei's original Mazinger Z TV series. The start of each file name is the Japanese episode number, followed by a letter signifying the dub at hand. Clear and easy to browse through, compared to previous uploads of these episodes. H = Hawaiian Dub, produced by M&M Communications at the request of Toei. No edits, most of the original music and character names, features Isao Sasaki's infamous English renditions of the vocal songs.  This version eventually reached the mainland US as a segment on the foreign affairs program Beyond the Horizon, along with a UK release containing four episodes. P = Phillipines Dub. Same approach as the Hawaiian dub, but with a different voice cast and none of the english vocal songs, instead opting for Japanese or instrumental versions. U = Tranzor Z, created for Mainland USA distribution by 3B Productions. Some things have been edited down/rewritten to make it past Standards and Practices, but the core of the series still shines through. Also features new music, sound effects and character names.
