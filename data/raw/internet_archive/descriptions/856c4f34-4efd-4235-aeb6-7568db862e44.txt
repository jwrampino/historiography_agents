One man who is today considered a hero inside of The Philippines is Jose Rizal, a writer who also worked as a doctor. He was a famous ophthalmologist and he wrote many novels which criticised the Spanish government who were in control of the Philippines. His ideas caused chaos with the Spanish who believed he was trying to incite revolution, and this eventually did break out. It became very dangerous for Jose Rizal to return home to his homeland, but he did this despite the threat of execution. But the Spanish authorities would catch up with him. He tried to travel to Cuba however was captured by the Spanish and he was then arrested and taken back to Manila. Whilst here was then sentenced to death for treason and sedition. In front of a huge crowd and a firing squad, he was executed by his own people and his death shocked many. Today Jose Rizal is considered a heroic figure in the Philippines and a cultural icon who's words helped to spark a revolution.
