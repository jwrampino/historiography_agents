Developed by Sphere, Inc. Platform DOS Published by Spectrum Holobyte, Inc. Released 1990 Genre Racing / Driving, Simulation Gameplay Tricks / Stunts, Vehicle Simulator Perspective 1st-person Vehicular Automobile Description Stunt Driver allows the player to control a '60s Mustang car and take on three different opponents in a virtually unlimited amount of courses (thanks to the included course editor). The player's driving style must be adjusted according to the opponent's behavior. Drawbridges, loops, banked curves, hills and oil slicks are there to impede the player-controlled car's progress. The game also features a replay camera complete with custom camera placement. From Mobygames.com. Original Entry
