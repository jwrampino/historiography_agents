This recording was made at The Station Night Club on February 20, 2003 by the late Matthew Pickett through a DAT recorder carried on his person. Much of the original 32 minute recording was released on February 24, 2025 by the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, and Firearms through a Freedom of Information Act request, although two small sections of audio was redacted under exemptions (b)(6) and (b)(7)(C). This is a digital reconstruction and remaster of the full recording using a variety of sources, including two 2025 FOIA releases from ATF, a portion of the audio played by author John Barylick at a 2018 authors' convention, and a four minute .wav section at the end of the recording obtained through a 2022 FOIA lawsuit by Ken McWatters provided to me for the restoration. In the reconstruction, there are still three sections of missing audio. The first two silent spots are sections still unreleased by ATF due to the aforementioned FOIA exemptions. The third silent spot is a splice that had to be physically put together when the tape was discovered in 2005. Listener discretion is strongly advised due to the graphic nature of the content.
