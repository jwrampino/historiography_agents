Oil & Gas Journal is an active weekly trade publication currently published by Endeavour Business Media. Launched in 1902 as the Oil Investor's Journal, it acquired its current name in 1910 and reports on news and developments in the petroleum industry. Each issue features news pieces on the oil and gas industry, analysis of current issues and events, and statistics on energy markets and industry activity. It also publishes information and updates on practical technology for design, operation, and maintenance of oil and gas operations. The journal is aimed at engineers, engineering managers, scientists, planners, or analysts who are employed in operating oil and gas companies. The Internet Archive Collection contains microfilm published between 1902 and 2016.
