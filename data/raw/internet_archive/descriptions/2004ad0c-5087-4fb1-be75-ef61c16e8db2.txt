Here's a rare treat, a concert by the last band featuring Jerry Garcia with Merl Saunders, Reconstruction . This band was founded by bassist John Kahn with Merl, and Garcia participated when he was available. This is the only band I'm aware of where Jerry was more of a side man than the main attraction. This concert from Rancho Nicasio in San Rafael was recorded by KPFA-FM in Berkeley (a Pacific Radio station) in March 1979, and may or may not have been broadcast at that time or a later date.  Reconstruction:   Merl Saunders - Keyboards, Vocals  Jerry Garcia - Guitar, Vocals  John Kahn - Bass Ron Stallings - Tenor Sax, Vocals Ed Neumeister - Trombone Gaylord Birch - Drums This band was short lived, read more about Reconstruction here:  https://www.upfullife.com/revisiting-reconstruction-jerry-garcias-short-lived-bay-area-jazz-funk-squad-from-1979-b-getz-on-l4lm/ Listen to more Garcia & Saunders broadcast recordings on FM Radio Archive : https://archive.org/details/fmradioarchive?tab=collection&query=garcia+saunders
