This episode of KOOP Radio's People United  presents coverage of the Rally to Stop Asian Hate, organized in Austin, Texas, by the local chapter of Asian Pacific Islander American Public Affairs in conjunction with other grassroots organizations in Central Texas, and featuring the voices of organizers, advocates, elected officials, and volunteers.  Steve Adler, Saatvik Ahulwalia, Awais Azhar, Colette Pierce Burnette, Greg Casar, Ashley Cheng, Sheryl Cole, Becca DeFelice, Vikki Goodwin, Stella Kim, Darlene Lanham, Jenni Lee, Hugh Li, Nelson Linder, Anna Nguyen, Rio Renaud, Pooja Sethi, James Talarico, Quynhanh Tran, and Lily Trieu spoke at Huston-Tillotson University on the city's east side on April 17, 2021.
