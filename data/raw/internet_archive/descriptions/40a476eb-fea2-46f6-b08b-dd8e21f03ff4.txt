Signatures: [A]-[B]² C² D² (-D2) Title from caption and first part of text Contains extensive biographical and genealogical information for an unidentified son of Juan Niño de Tabora (Governor of the Philippines from 1626-1632) and Magdalena de Saldibar y Mendoza. The unnamed individual whose political career is described in this source was orphaned at the age of ten months and left the Phillipines for Mexico City in 1643. According to this source, Marcos de Torres Rueda (Bishop of Yucatan), granted this unnamed individual a merced over the Alcaldia Mayor of Xicayan Province and its jurrisdiction and in 1648, Justlahuaca (Oaxaca?), and he was Alcalde Mayor of Antequera (Oaxaca) in 1652. Other individuals mentioned in this source include: Antonio Niño, Luis de Silba, Juan de Silba y Ribera, Ana Bañuelos y Mendoza, Lorenzo de Olazo y Achotigui, Sebastian Hurtado de Corquera and Felipe Hernandez Machado Dated at the end: Mexico, y Mar̦̦c̦o 3. de 1652 Medina, J.T.
