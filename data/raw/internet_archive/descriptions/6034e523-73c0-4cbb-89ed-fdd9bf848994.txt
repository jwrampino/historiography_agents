World Oil is an active publication covering the oil and gas industry. It was established in 1916 and is published monthly by Gulf Publishing Co. The publication aims to present the latest information for personnel engaged in oil and gas exploration, drilling, and production. Its content is tailored to provide engineering and operating ideas and interpretations of significant trends to industry decision-makers—operating management, independent producers, drilling contractors, production engineers, drilling engineers, and purchasing personnel.
