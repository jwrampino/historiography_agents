Ken Park is a 2002 erotic drama which revolves around the abusive and dysfunctional lives of several teenagers, set in the city of Visalia, California. It was written by Harmony Korine, who based it on Larry Clark's journals and stories. The film was directed and shot by Clark and Edward Lachman.  Release date: 3 April 2003 (Netherlands) Directors: Larry Clark, Ed Lachman Budget: 1.3 million USD Cinematography: Larry Clark, Ed Lachman Story by: Larry Clark Casting director: Carmen Cuba
