Russia-1 &nbsp;( Russian :&nbsp; Россия-1 ) is a state-owned&nbsp; Russian &nbsp; television channel , &nbsp;first aired on 14 February 1956 as&nbsp; Programme Two &nbsp;in the&nbsp; Soviet Union . It was relaunched as RTR on 13 May 1991, and is known today as Russia-1. It is the flagship channel of the&nbsp; All-Russia State Television and Radio Company &nbsp;(VGTRK)
