The World Rainforest Report was a magazine produced by Australia's Rainforest Information Centre as a print publication from 1984 to 1997. It included reports and information about the state of rainforests across the globe as well as news about activism concerning their protection. From 1997 to 2004 the magazine appeared online. Since then news updates have been regularly added to www.rainforestinfo.org.au This issue of the magazine came out in 1987 and its contents included: Penan Struggle Continues India Update Coca Cola Cans Rainforests Third World Debt- An Ecological Solution World Bank campaign update Report on World Resources Institute conferences Earth First film debut Bestiary poem News and correspondence from Brazil, Ecuador, USA, Sri Lanka, Switzerland, Canada, Phillipines, Jamaica Report Invocation from Council of All Beings Rainforest Rock lyrics Green Tax report Deforestation and Desertification Control Network Formed
