Annals of Plastic Surgery is a publication covering plastic and reconstructive surgery. It was established in 1978 and is published monthly in the United States by Lippincott Williams & Wilkins. The journal features research articles, case reports, reviews, and clinical studies that cover a wide range of topics within the field, including craniofacial reconstruction, burn management, microsurgery, aesthetic procedures, reconstructive techniques, and innovations in surgical instrumentation. Its scope encompasses both clinical and experimental research, emphasizing advancements in surgical techniques, outcomes, and fundamental science related to tissue transfer, wound healing, and implantology. The publication is targeted at surgeons, researchers, and clinicians dedicated to the continual evolution of plastic surgery practices worldwide. Annals of Plastic Surgery can be found online at: https://journals.lww.com/annalsplasticsurgery
