These materials, the originals of which are in the PCOM Library , were digitized in 2010 with funding provided by the Library and the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation through the Lyrasis Mass Digitization Collaborative. Scanning and OCR were performed by, and derivative formats created by, the Internet Archive. PCOM Department of Alumni Relations & Development was a valued collaborator. This project provides access to the entire span of available PCOM college yearbooks. Yearbooks are not available for the years 1943, 1945-1949, 1951-1953, and 1955. The Library will gratefully accept donations of copies of any missing years. There are no copyright restrictions on use of these materials for non-commercial purposes.
