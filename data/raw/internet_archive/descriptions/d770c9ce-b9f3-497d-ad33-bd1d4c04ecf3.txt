LibriVox recording of Dere Mable, by Edward Streeter. Read by Rob Kunkel. Bill is in training camp, preparing to go off to World War I. This book is a collection of love letters written to his sweetheart, Mable. The letters are humorous, mis-spelled, and have many stories of life in an army camp - all from Bill's unique perspective.
