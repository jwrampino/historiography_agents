The Venezuelan Elections 2012 web archive preserves various iterations of the webpages of four Venezuelan political parties leading up to and immediately following the scheduled inauguration of the incumbent president Hugo Chávez on January 10, 2013. The inauguration was postponed as Chávez was receiving cancer treatment in Cuba, and he died on March 5, 2013 before taking office. The four parties and date range of website captures includes the Gran Polo Patriótico (captured 36 times between June 3, 2012 and Feb. 9, 2013), Partido Socialista Unido de Venezuela (captured 37 times between May 31, 2012 and Feb. 7, 2013), Primero Justicia (captured 36 times between June 3, 2012 and Feb. 2, 2013), and Unidad Venezuela (captured 38 times between May 30, 2012 and Feb. 13, 2013).
