Open American Standard Version The Revivalist's Note Taking Edition The Open American Standard Version (OASV) is an updated version of the famous American Standard Version of 1912. The difference is that archaic language has been replaced with modern equivalences by a computer program to make it more readable by a modern audience. The OASV is in the public domain which makes it perfect for creating derivative works. The note taking edition allows readers to have ample space for writing notes such as sermons, key concepts, ideas and revelations.
